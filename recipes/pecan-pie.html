Aunt Joy's Pecan Pie

From papa Terry's sister.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs slightly beaten
  • 1 c Karo light
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 c pecans
  • 1 pie shell

Recipe

Mix together, adding nuts last. Pour into unbaked pie shell.

Bake at 450° for 10 min. Reduce heat to 350° and bake until done.
