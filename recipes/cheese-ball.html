Cheese Ball

Ingredients

  • (2) 8 oz pkg cream cheese
  • (2) 3 oz pkg corned beef
  • med onion
  • 1 tbls worcestershire
  • -1/2 tbls horseradish

Recipe

se 1 pkg corned beef chopped fine and mix with onion, worcestershire and horseradish. Form into a ball and roll in the pkg of finely chopped corn beef.

Chill and serve.
