Hollow Rye Bread Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 lb loaf of rye bread (unsliced)
  • (2) 1 lb loaves of rye bread (unsliced)

Hollow out the center of the larger loaf.

  • 3 pkgs Nippy sharp cheese spread
  • 1 pkg blue cheese
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 med onion chopped
  • 1 tbls worcestershire sauce
  • beer to thin to proper consistency

Recipe

Mix all ingredients.

Cube remaining loaves and use for dipping.
