Buckeyes

Ingredients

  • 2 c peanut butter
  • 1/2 c butter
  • 6 c powdered sugar

Mix above and roll into balls.

  • 12 oz chocolate chips
  • 1/3 bar of parafin wax

Melt together in a double boiler.

Recipe

Dip balls into chocolate using a toothpick and place on foil to dry. Refrigerate.
