Christmas Wreaths

Ingredients

  • (50) 1 g marshmallows
  • 1/4 c butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tsp green food coloring
  • 5 c corn flakes

Recipe

Melt marshmallows and butter in double boiler. Add vanilla food coloring.

Butter hands and bowl! Pour melted mixture over corn flakes. Stir with a well buttered fork 2-3 times. Shape into wreaths and place on wax paper. Let stand 24-36 hrs. Decorate with red candies.
