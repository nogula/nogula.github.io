Linguini w/ White Clam Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c olive oil
  • 2 cans clams minced, drained (reserve juice)
  • 2 tbls minced garlic
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • S&P
  • additional clam juice

Recipe

Saute clams in olive oil. Add garlic, reserved and additional (one small bottle) clam juice, oregano, basil, salt, pepper to taste.

Toss w/ linguini.
