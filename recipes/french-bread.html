French Bread

This is my dad's recipe, which I think is based on his dad's recipe. My dad would make this bread at most holidays. My brothers and I would always request it.

Ingredients

  • 2 c flour
  • 1 tbls salt
  • 1 tbls sugar
  • 2 pkgs dry yeast
  • 2-1/4 cup hot tap water

Recipe

Mix together the dry ingredients. Add the water and mix with a mixer. Add 2-3 more cups of flour until the mixer won't handle it anymore and begin using a spoon. Turn onto a board and knead in flour until smooth and elastic, slightly sticky.

Let rise until double in size. Punch down & shape into 2 loaves.
