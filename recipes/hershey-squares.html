Hershey Squares

Grandma Jackie always made these each year around christmas. I would go nuts for them as a kid.

Recipe

Cream together:

  • 1/2 c butter
  • 1 c sugar
  • 4 eggs

Beat well and add to above:

  • 16 oz Hershey's syrup
  • 1 c flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Pour mixture into ungreased 9x13. Bake at 350Â° for 30 min.

Combine and beat well:

  • 1/2 c margarine
  • 2 c powdered sugar
  • 2 tbls creme de menthe

Pour above mixture over cooked cake and refrigerate for 2 hrs or until set.

Melt and pour over 2nd layer, then refrigerate:

  • (1) 6 oz pkg chocolate chips.
  • 6 tbls margaine

Cut into 1" squares. Makes about 60 pieces.
