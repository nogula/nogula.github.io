Cream Cheese Chicken

This was a staple growing up. Dad says to keep it simple, don't modify.

Ingredients

  • (1) 8 oz pkg cream cheese
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 lb bacon
  • 8 chicken breasts

Recipe

Mix onion and cheese.

Pound boneless chicken breasts flat. Spread w/ cheese mixture.

Roll & wrap in a slice of bacon.

Bake at 325Â° till done.
