7 Layer Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 stick melted butter
  • 1 c crushed graham crackers
  • 1 c coconut
  • (1) 6 oz pkg chocolate chips
  • (1) 6 oz pkg butter scotch chips
  • 1-1/2 c chopped walnuts
  • 1 can condensed sweetened milk

Recipe

Melt butter in 9x13 pan and add rest of ingredients in order. DO NOT STIR. Sprinkle milk over all and bake at 325Â° for 30 min. Cut into small squares.
