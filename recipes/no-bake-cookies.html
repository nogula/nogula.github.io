No Bake Cookies

Ingredients

  • 4 c sugar
  • 1/4 c butter
  • 1 c milk
  • 1 c loosely packed cocoa
  • 1-1/2 c peanut butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 6 c quick cook oats

Recipe

Boil 10 minutes the sugar, butter, and milk. Remove from heat and add the oats. Combine cocoa, peanut butter, and vanilla and add to oat mixture.

Drop by spoon on wax paper. Let stand 1 hr.
