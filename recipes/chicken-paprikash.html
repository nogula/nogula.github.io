Chicken Paprikash

Serves 4. This is my dad's favorite meal!! (He added the 2x exclamation marks.) My dad usually served it with spaetzle

Ingredients

  • 2 tbls bacon grease
  • 1 lg onion chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic chopped
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3+ tbls paprika
  • 3 lb chicken thighs
  • 1 c water
  • (1) 14 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbls flour
  • (1) 8 oz pkg sour cream

Recipe

Saute onion, garlic, red peper flakes and paprika in bacon grease.

Cook chicken over med heat for 1 hour adding more water if necessary.

Stir in tomatoes (reserve juice). Mix flour, sour cream + rserved tomato juice in a medium bowl then add to chicken stirring constantly.

Serve over spaetzle.
