Crispy Fries

Ingredients

  • russet potatoes (or other potato with high starch)

Recipe

Peel potatoes, soak in water for 10 mins. Boil potatoes until tender, but not crumbling. Remove water. Toss in S&P. Rough up the edges a bit with a fork. Cook in air fryer until crispy. Probably have to flip once or twice.
