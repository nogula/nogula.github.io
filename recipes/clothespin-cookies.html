Clothespin Cookies

Ingredients

Dough

Mix and refigerate overnight:

  • 2-1/2 c flour
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 sticks margarine
  • 1/2 pt sourcream

Filling

Cook together. Stir to make a paste. COOL.

  • 4-1/2 tbls flour
  • 1-1/2 c milk

Cream together then add below ingredients:

  • 3/4 c crisco
  • 3/4 c butter

Beat well then add 1-1/2 c sugar, 1-1/2 tsp vanilla. Then add cooled paste mixture.

Beat filling mixture until creamy then add 3/4 c powdered sugar.

Recipe

Separate dough into quarters. Roll out 1/4" thick on floured surface as if it were a pie dough. Cut into 1x5 inch strips. Wrap around clean wooden clothespins.

Bake at 350Â° about 8 mins. Remove clothespin before completely cooled.

Using a pastry bag, fill each cookie.
