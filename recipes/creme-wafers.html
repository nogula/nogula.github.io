Creme Wafers

Ingredients

For cookies:

  • 1 c soft butter
  • 1/2 c whipping cream
  • 2 c sifted flour

For filling:

  • 1/2 c butter
  • 1-1/2 c sifted powdered sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp fanilla
  • Food coloring to tint

Recipe

For cookies, mix butter, whipping cream, and flour. Chill thoroughly.

Roll out 1/5 of dough at a time. Keep the rest of the dough refrigerated. Roll dough to 1/3" thick on sugared surface with 1-1/2" round cookie cutter. Prick with a fork 4 times. Bake 7-9 min at 350Â°.

Combine filling ingredients and assemble cookies.

You must work fast when rolling out dough!
