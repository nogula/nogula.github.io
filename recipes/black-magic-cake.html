Black Magic Cake

My dad's favorite cake. He doesn't like icing, so he says the icing is optional.

Ingredients

  • 1-3/4 c flour
  • 2 c sugar
  • 3/4 c cocoa
  • 2 tsp soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • *1 c sour skim milk
  • 1/2 c oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
*Sour milk w 1 tbls vinegar in a cup and fill with skim milk. Let stand 15 min.

Recipe

Combine all ingredients. Dry first then wet and beat 2 min.

Pour into a 9x13 greased pan.

Bake at 350Â° 30-35 min.
