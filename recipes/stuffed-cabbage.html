Stuffed Cabbage

Filling

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 c raw rice
  • 1 med onion diced
  • 2 eggs
  • S&P
  • garlic powder
  • sourkraut
Mix above ingredients.

Recipe

Steam head of cabbage until leaves wilt off (remove them as they wilt!). Stuff and roll each leaf.

Put sourkraut on bottom and top of kettle. Fill kettle w/ stuffed cabbage, pour cabbage water over top Layer tomator sauce in between layers of rolls.

Mix hot sauce with sour cream and pour over.

Cook until done.
