ettucini Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • mushrooms
  • 1 pint half n half
  • S&P
  • 1/2 c parmesean

Recipe

Cook, drain, and butter fettucini.

Saute garlic and mushrooms on med heat (don't burn garlic).

Add half n half.

Salt, pepper, and parmesean at the very end.

Toss w/ fettucini.
