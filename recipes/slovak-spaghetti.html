Slovak Spaghetti

From great grandma Gula.

Ingredients

  • (2) sticks butter
  • (1) large onion
  • (2) tbls chopped garlic
  • (1) tbls sugar
  • S&P
  • (1) 12 oz can of tomato sauce
  • (1) lb cooked thin spaghetti

Recipe

Cook butter over med high heat until it turns light brown. Add onions, garlic, and saute quickly. (careful not to burn the garlic!)

Add the rest of the ingredients. Turn down heat and cook for 20 minutes. Mix with spaghetti.
