Christmas Soup

My dad made this every Christmas. It is a tradition. I'm told that it is mandatory.

Recipe

Brown 1 lb butter and 2 large onions (chopped fine).

Add (4) 16 oz cans sourkraut (drained dry, chopped fine, reserve juice).

Cook over med heat until soukraut is browned. Add half of sourkraut juice and water to make 3/4 kettle.

Add 2 handfulls of raw rice. Simmer 1 hour. Add (4) 8 oz cans sliced mushrooms and juice. S&P to taste.

My dad likes to make it the day before. It gets better with time.
