Barbecue Beef

Ingredients

  • 3 lb chuck roast
  • 1 btl Heinz chili sauce
  • (1) 2 L btl gingerale
  • oil

Recipe

Remove all fat and bone from roast. Brown roast pieces in hot oil. Pour over chili sauce and cover with gingerale. Simmer 2-3 hhrs covered until meat shreds easily.

Boil off most of liquid and serve.
