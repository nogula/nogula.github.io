Strawberry Pie

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • Sugar to taste
  • baked pie crust
  • strawberries
  • 1-1/2 c sugar
  • 5 tbls cornstarch
  • 2 c seven-up
  • (1) 3 oz pkg strawberry jello

Recipe

Cook sugar, coornstarch, seven up until clear and thick. Stir in strawberry jell. Chill for one hour.

Coat bottom of pie crust with the cream cheese and sugar mixture.

Add strawberries to jello mixture and place in pie shell.
