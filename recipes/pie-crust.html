Grandma Gula's Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 7 c flour
  • 1 tbls salt
  • 1 lb lard
  • 1 c water (+ 2 tbls if needed)

Recipe

Mix as you would any pie crust.

Mix flour and salt, add lard, and use pastry belnder. Add water and mix a little with the pastry blender then hands. Chill dough. Roll out. Etc.
