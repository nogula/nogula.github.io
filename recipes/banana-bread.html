Aunt Joy's Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 c sugar
  • 1 c butter
  • 4 eggs beaten
  • 4 c flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1-1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 c mashed banana
  • 1 c nuts
  • 1 tsp vannilla

Recipe

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat well. Add flour, soda, powder (sifeted) alternately with bananas. Add nuts and vanilla last.
