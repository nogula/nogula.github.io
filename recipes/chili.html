Chili

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs lean ground beef
  • 2 med onions chopped
  • 2-3 tbls garlic chopped
  • (2) 28 oz cans tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper
  • (2) 8 oz can mushrooms drained
  • (2) 16 oz can kidney beans
  • S&P
  • 4+ tbls chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Recipe

Brown the ground beef and drain fat. Add the rest - break up the tomatoes and simmer 1-2 hrs.
