Italian Spaghetti Sauce

This recipe is from my dad. It is the red sauce used to make shrimp + cream sauce, which his friend Brad renamed "Shrimp w/ Aaron Sauce."

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs country spare ribs
  • (1) 28 oz can tomator puree
  • (1) 12 oz can tomator paste
  • 6 oz red wine
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 2 tbls oregano
  • 1 tbls basil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbls sugar
  • salt
  • olive oil

Recipe

Brown the country ribs in hot oil. Add everything else and cook for 2-3 hours. (Add water if too thick.)

Remove the ribs and eat them!

This is a very good basic italian sauce to use on its own, with meatballs, or as a base to go from!
