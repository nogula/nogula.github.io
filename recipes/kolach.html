Kolach

Ingredients

  • 1 qt milk
  • 3 c sugar
  • 2 tbls salt
  • 1 lb butter
  • 5 lbs +++ flour
  • 10 pkg dry yeast
  • 10 beaten eggs

Recipe

Scald milk with next three ingredients. When mixture cools to 100-115° add yeast and mix. Add eggs and mix. Add flour until it can be turned onto a board and knead. Knead until smooth and elastic. Let rise until double. Divide into 8 parts.

Roll out into large rectangle to fit a large cookie sheet. Filling is spread out and then you roll up and seal edge w/ water.

(3 rolls per large cookie sheet.)

Bake at 375° 25-30 min.

This is the dough recipe for the sweet bread great grandma Gula made at the holidays. It is one of my dad's favorites.

Fillings

  • 4 lbs nuts
  • 5 c sugar
  • white rasins

Add boiling water till spreadable.

Poppyseed

  • 2 lb poppy seed
  • 7 c sugar
  • 3 c water

Combine and let boil covered 5-10 min. Careful: burns easily.

Cheese

  • 2 lb ricotta
  • 1 c sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tsp flour
  • yellow raisins

Add raisins to cheese and let stand 1 hour. Mix in all ingredients and use.

Lekvar (prune)

Add sugar and cinnamon to taste.
