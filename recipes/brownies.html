Grandpa Terry's Brownies

Ingredients

  • 3-3/4 c flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 5/8 tsp baking powder
  • 5/8 tsp salt
  • 7 c of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 3-1/3 c sugar
  • 3 sticks + 3 tbls butter
  • 3/4 c corn sugar
  • 3/4 c water
  • 9 eggs
  • 3 tbls vanilla

Recipe

On stove mix sugar, butter, corn syrup, and water. Stir constantly until boiling. Pour over chocolate chips in large bowl and let set for 2 min. With mixer add eggs and vanilla. Add dry mixture.

Bake in 2 11x17 lined pans at 325Â° for 35 min. At 17 min, switch racks. Cookie sheets work best.

Add caramel or nuts if you want.
